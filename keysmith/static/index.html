    Paper Backup Checklist

    • Use a clean offline machine and verify the app before generating keys.
    • Write secrets by hand or use an offline printer with no storage or networking.
    • Verify the written WIF, nsec, or raw private key hex below before deleting anything.
    • Store duplicate copies separately; paper can be lost, damaged, or misread.
    • Wipe the machine/storage properly. Deleting the app does not erase memory, logs, swap, screenshots, or backups.

    Verify Backup

    Paste a WIF, nsec, or raw private key hex while offline to derive the public address or npub and compare it with your paper backup.
